BENEFIT RULES — Blue Shield Insurance PPO-2024 Plan
Version: 2024.1
Effective: January 1, 2024

COVERED PROCEDURES
The following procedure codes are covered under PPO-2024:
- 27447: Total knee arthroplasty (knee replacement) — covered for musculoskeletal indications only
- 27446: Hemi knee arthroplasty — covered
- 22630: Lumbar posterior interbody arthrodesis (spinal fusion) — covered for spinal indications
- 22632: Lumbar posterior interbody arthrodesis additional level — covered as add-on to 22630
- 99213: Office visit, established patient, moderate complexity — covered
- 99214: Office visit, established patient, high complexity — covered
- 71046: Chest X-ray, 2 views — covered

EXCLUDED PROCEDURES
- Cosmetic procedures are excluded unless reconstructive and documented as medically necessary.
- Experimental procedures not listed in the CPT-2024 manual are excluded.
- Procedures performed outside network without emergency authorization are excluded.

DEDUCTIBLE
- Annual deductible: $2,000 per member per calendar year
- Deductible applies before coinsurance kicks in
- Deductible resets January 1 of each year

COINSURANCE
- After deductible is met: Plan pays 80%, Member pays 20%
- Coinsurance applies to contracted rate, not billed amount
- Example: If contracted rate is $7,200 and deductible is met, plan pays $5,760 and member pays $1,440

OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM
- Annual out-of-pocket maximum: $5,000 per member per calendar year
- Once out-of-pocket maximum is reached, plan pays 100% of covered services for remainder of year

NETWORK REQUIREMENTS
- Services must be rendered by a contracted provider unless emergency
- Valley Orthopedic Center (NPI: 1234567893) is a contracted provider
- Regional Spine Center (NPI: 2345678904) is a contracted provider
- Quick Medical Clinic (NPI: 3456789015) is NOT a contracted provider

COORDINATION OF BENEFITS
- PPO-2024 is primary for all members without other active insurance
- Secondary claims require Explanation of Benefits from primary carrier

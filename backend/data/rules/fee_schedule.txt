FEE SCHEDULE — Blue Shield Insurance Contracted Rates
Version: 2024.1
Effective: January 1, 2024

PURPOSE
These are the contracted reimbursement rates negotiated between Blue Shield Insurance and network providers. Payment is calculated based on contracted rate, not billed amount. The contracted rate is the allowed amount before deductible and coinsurance calculations.

PAYMENT CALCULATION EXAMPLE
Claim: 27447 at Valley Orthopedic Center
Billed amount: $8,500.00
Contracted rate (allowed amount): $7,200.00
Assumed deductible already met for year: $0 remaining
Coinsurance: Plan pays 80%, Member pays 20%
Plan payment: $7,200.00 x 0.80 = $5,760.00
Member payment: $7,200.00 x 0.20 = $1,440.00

PAYMENT CALCULATION EXAMPLE WITH DEDUCTIBLE
Claim: 27447 at Valley Orthopedic Center
Billed amount: $8,500.00
Contracted rate (allowed amount): $7,200.00
Remaining deductible: $2,000.00
Member pays deductible first: $2,000.00
Remaining after deductible: $7,200.00 - $2,000.00 = $5,200.00
Coinsurance on remainder: Plan pays 80% = $4,160.00, Member pays 20% = $1,040.00
Total member responsibility: $2,000.00 + $1,040.00 = $3,040.00
Total plan payment: $4,160.00

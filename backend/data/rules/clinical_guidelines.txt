CLINICAL GUIDELINES — Procedure to Diagnosis Matching
Blue Shield Insurance — Medical Policy Department
Version: 2024.1
Effective: January 1, 2024

PURPOSE
These guidelines define which ICD-10 diagnosis codes clinically support each covered procedure code. A procedure billed without a clinically appropriate diagnosis is considered a clinical mismatch and must be denied regardless of other factors.

PROCEDURE 27447 — TOTAL KNEE ARTHROPLASTY
Supported diagnosis codes (PASS):
- M17.0x: Bilateral primary osteoarthritis of knee
- M17.11: Primary osteoarthritis, right knee
- M17.12: Primary osteoarthritis, left knee
- M17.31: Post-traumatic osteoarthritis, right knee
- M17.32: Post-traumatic osteoarthritis, left knee
- M17.5: Other secondary osteoarthritis of knee
- M17.9: Osteoarthritis of knee, unspecified
- M25.361: Stiffness of right knee
- M25.362: Stiffness of left knee

Diagnoses that do NOT support 27447 (FAIL — automatic denial if primary diagnosis):
- Any J-code (respiratory conditions: pneumonia, bronchitis, asthma, COPD, etc.)
- Any cardiac codes (I-codes: heart failure, MI, arrhythmia)
- Any metabolic codes (E-codes: diabetes, thyroid unless direct knee complication documented)
- Any infectious codes (A-codes, B-codes) unless septic arthritis specifically documented
- Any mental health codes (F-codes)

Clinical note: J18.9 (Pneumonia, unspecified) with procedure 27447 (Total knee arthroplasty) is clinically impossible as a primary claim. Pneumonia is a pulmonary condition with no clinical pathway to knee replacement surgery. This combination indicates billing error or fraud.

PROCEDURE 22630 — LUMBAR POSTERIOR INTERBODY ARTHRODESIS
Supported diagnosis codes (PASS):
- M43.16: Spondylolysis, lumbar region
- M43.17: Spondylolysis, lumbosacral region
- M51.16: Intervertebral disc degeneration, lumbar region
- M51.17: Intervertebral disc degeneration, lumbosacral region
- M47.816: Spondylosis with radiculopathy, lumbar region
- M47.817: Spondylosis with radiculopathy, lumbosacral region
- M54.4: Lumbago with sciatica

Diagnoses that do NOT support 22630 (FAIL):
- Any knee-specific codes (M17.x, M25.3x without spinal documentation)
- Any respiratory, cardiac, metabolic conditions as primary diagnosis
- Diagnoses limited to cervical or thoracic spine without lumbar involvement

PROCEDURE 22632 — ADD-ON SPINAL FUSION LEVEL
- Only valid as an add-on to 22630
- Cannot be billed without 22630 on the same date of service
- Supported diagnoses are the same as for 22630

GENERAL CLINICAL MATCHING RULE
A primary diagnosis must directly and clinically justify the primary procedure. Supporting diagnoses may be present but do not override a clinically impossible primary pairing. When the primary diagnosis and primary procedure have no established clinical pathway connecting them, the claim must be denied for clinical mismatch.
